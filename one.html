🏆 10 تعبيرات أمريكية قوية

تمام يا زعيم 😎🔥
هنا 10 تعبيرات أمريكية قوية، وكل تعبير مع 4 أمثلة مختلفة، يعني هتطلع معاك 50 جملة جاهزة للحفظ والتطبيق فورًا.

1️⃣ I’m all about...
كل اهتمامي بـ / أنا بحب جدًا
  • I’m all about trying new foods. 🍣
  • I’m all about helping others. 🤝
  • I’m all about staying healthy. 🥗
  • I’m all about learning new things. 📚
2️⃣ I can’t get enough of...
مش بشبع من / مدمن على
  • I can’t get enough of coffee. ☕
  • I can’t get enough of traveling. ✈️
  • I can’t get enough of your jokes. 😂
  • I can’t get enough of music. 🎵
3️⃣ I’m the kind of person who...
أنا من النوع اللي
  • I’m the kind of person who wakes up early. ⏰
  • I’m the kind of person who plans ahead. 📝
  • I’m the kind of person who loves surprises. 🎁
  • I’m the kind of person who asks a lot of questions. ❓
4️⃣ If I had to choose, I’d go for...
لو لازم أختار، هاختار
  • If I had to choose, I’d go for pizza. 🍕
  • If I had to choose, I’d go for the beach. 🏖️
  • If I had to choose, I’d go for staying home. 🏠
  • If I had to choose, I’d go for a quiet night. 🌙
5️⃣ What I love most is...
أكثر شيء أحبه هو
  • What I love most is spending time with friends. 👫
  • What I love most is the smell of fresh bread. 🍞
  • What I love most is watching the sunset. 🌅
  • What I love most is learning something new every day. 📖
6️⃣ I can’t imagine my life without...
ما أقدرش أتخيل حياتي من غير
  • I can’t imagine my life without coffee. ☕
  • I can’t imagine my life without my phone. 📱
  • I can’t imagine my life without traveling. 🌍
  • I can’t imagine my life without good friends. 🤗
7️⃣ One thing I always make time for is...
حاجة دايمًا بفضل أعملها
  • One thing I always make time for is reading. 📖
  • One thing I always make time for is exercise. 🏋️‍♂️
  • One thing I always make time for is family. 👨‍👩‍👧
  • One thing I always make time for is coffee in the morning. ☕
8️⃣ I’m not really into...
مش بحب أوي
  • I’m not really into horror movies. 🎬
  • I’m not really into spicy food. 🌶️
  • I’m not really into big parties. 🎉
  • I’m not really into shopping. 🛍️
9️⃣ I’d rather...
أفضل أن
  • I’d rather stay home tonight. 🏠
  • I’d rather take the train than the bus. 🚆
  • I’d rather read a book than watch TV. 📚
  • I’d rather go for a walk. 🚶‍♂️

بالنسبة لي، الموضوع كله عن

  • For me, it’s all about learning new things. 📖
  • For me, it’s all about staying positive. 😊

🗣️ تمبلتس الحكي اليومي (Story Mode)

🌅 الصبح
القالب الترجمة مثال
So, this morning I woke up and… فـ النهارده الصبح صحيت و… So, this morning I woke up and checked my phone.
The first thing I did was… أول حاجة عملتها كانت… The first thing I did was make coffee.
I usually start my day by… عادة ببدأ يومي بـ… I usually start my day by stretching.
🍳 الفطار والاستعداد
القالب الترجمة مثال
Then I went ahead and… بعدين عملت و… Then I went ahead and made breakfast.
I felt like… كان نفسي في… I felt like having some toast.
While I was getting ready… وأنا بجهز… While I was getting ready, I listened to music.
🏢 الشغل أو الدراسة
القالب الترجمة مثال
After that, I headed to… بعد كده رُحت… After that, I headed to work.
I spent most of my morning… قضيت معظم الصبح… I spent most of my morning replying to emails.
Around midday, I… حوالي نص النهار، أنا… Around midday, I had lunch.
🌇 بعد الظهر
القالب الترجمة مثال
Later in the afternoon, I… بعدين في العصر… Later in the afternoon, I had a meeting.
I was thinking about… كنت بفكر في… I was thinking about going for a walk.
Instead, I decided to… بدل كده قررت… Instead, I decided to stay home.
🌙 نهاية اليوم
القالب الترجمة مثال
When I got home, I… لما رجعت البيت… When I got home, I made dinner.
I usually end my day by… غالبًا بخلص يومي بـ… I usually end my day by reading.
Before going to bed, I… قبل ما أنام… Before going to bed, I brushed my teeth.

📚 كلمات تساعدك توصف أحداثك اليومية

أفعال شائعة:
  • wake up (أصحى)
  • get up (أقوم من السرير)
  • make breakfast (أعمل فطار)
  • check my phone (أبص على الموبايل)
  • brush my teeth (أفرّش سناني)
  • take a shower (أستحمى)
  • get dressed (ألبس هدومي)
  • go to work/school (أروح الشغل/المدرسة)
  • have lunch (أتغدى)
  • take a nap (أخد قيلولة)
  • cook dinner (أطبخ عشاء)
  • watch TV (أتفرج على التلفزيون)
  • go for a walk (أتمشى)
  • go to bed (أروح أنام)
كلمات وقت:
  • in the morning (في الصبح)
  • in the afternoon (بعد الظهر)
  • in the evening (في المساء)
  • at night (بالليل)
  • around midday (حوالي نص النهار)
  • later on (بعدين)
  • finally (وأخيرًا)
